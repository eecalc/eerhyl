v1.6.13

Update by Reece:

  • Static Shift Goals (The Gamified Checklist): The Daily RR column is now a frozen shift goal! Instead of the number dropping as you make sales, the number is locked at the start of your shift. When you successfully hit the target for that KPI today, the number transforms into a satisfying green "✓". It resets automatically the next morning.
  • Crash Bug Terminated: Patched a critical logic flaw in the table renderer that caused the app to freeze when calculating the new Run Rate column.
  • Coach Minimise Toggle: Tap 'Minimise' to save screen space when you just want to view your raw numbers, and 'Maximise' when you want your strategy back.
