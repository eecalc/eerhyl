🚀 Release Notes

v1.4.7 (The Feature Consolidation)

Here is the complete list of EVERYTHING Reece has engineered into the application today:

  • Strict App Lockdown: The app completely freezes your coach, tracking, and projections until you update your Completed Shifts for the day to ensure math is never broken.
  • Custom Modal Warnings: Replaced ugly browser prompts with sleek, branded pop-ups for confirmations, shift locks, and admin passwords.
  • Confirm & Lock Mechanic: If you miss a day, you can adjust your shifts freely using both +/- or typing, and then click 'Confirm' to lock the app for the day.
  • Commission Maximizer (The Vault): When you hit 75%, the app switches into Maximizer Mode. It reveals exactly what you need to sell per shift to hit 100%, featuring golden progress bars and a satisfying 'Daily Pace Hit' tick.
  • Month Submission Lock: Added a 'Lock In' button for the start of the month to prevent accidental changes.
  • Setup Mode & Protection: Once you enter your total shifts, Setup Mode vanishes and locks your numbers so targets cannot be accidentally changed.
  • Anti-Duplication Coach: The Daily Coach engine has been mathematically rebuilt to ensure you are never given identical strategy options.
  • Intraday Shift Summary: A new table drops in showing exactly how many items you've banked *today* and the % they're worth.
  • Sticky Scoreboard: Your Current and Projected Overall % now follow you down the page so you can watch your score go up in real time.
  • UI Enhancements: Quick-add +/- buttons added to Targets. Table headers perfectly centered above inputs and progress bars.
  • Admin Dashboard: Secret backdoor access added via the EE logo for Management to bypass locks, run the Time Travel Simulator, and correct math without exposing passwords on the shop floor.
