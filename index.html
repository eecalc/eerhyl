🚀 Release Notes

v1.6.2 (26/03/2026)

Engineered and designed entirely by Reece:

  • Fatal Bug Terminated: Fixed the variable crash that was breaking the Catch-Up Strategy screen.
  • Static Coach & Merged Baskets: The Coach now locks your playbook in for the day (no more shrinking on every sale) and strictly caps options at 4 KPIs max, cleanly merging duplicates (e.g., "2 SIMs").
  • Clean Buffer Math: Removed confusing decimals. Accurately tracks whole buffer days, including negative shift debt.
  • The In-Memory Evaluator: Flawlessly tracks "Renegade" shift smashes and restored the Mid-Shift Promotion banner when you pull out of the red.
  • Streamlined UI & Clear Instructions: Removed the 'What If' table & Projected %, and clarified the shift-logging routine (log +1 every day for yesterday, even if off sick).
  • 🔥 Hot Streak Gamification: Added a dynamic streak counter for consecutive 'Smashed' shifts. Miss a shift, and it burns back to 0.
  • 🌙 Dark Mode: High-contrast dark theme toggle added to the top left.
  • Smart Background Refresh: App now detects if left open overnight and safely syncs to the new day automatically.
